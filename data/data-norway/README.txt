These datasets were created by combining data from air quality monitoring stations and from weather stations in Norway.

The source of PM2.5 data is: European Environment Agency (https://discomap.eea.europa.eu/map/fme/AirQualityExport.htm, https://www.eea.europa.eu/en/legal-notice)
The source of meteorological data is: The Norwegian Meteorological institute (https://frost.met.no/index.html, https://www.met.no/en/free-meteorological-data/Licensing-and-crediting)

All times are in UTC.
The weekday parameter indicates whether the corresponding day was a work day (Monday - Friday), Saturday, or Sunday/public holiday.
Units are: PM2.5 in µg/m³; temperature in °C; wind speed in m/s; rain in mm.

The measurements from the air quality monitoring stations (on the left) have been combined with the meteorological measurements from the following weather stations (on the right):
Valand: Sola
Knarrdalstranda: Porsgrunn (Ås)
Nygaardsgata: Øsaker
Klosterhaugen: Skredderdalen
Nedre-Langgate: Melsom
