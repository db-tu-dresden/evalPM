These datasets were created by averaging hourly air quality forecasts of each day, specifically the PM2.5 forecasts (in µg/m³) of the EURAD-IM model and of the ENSEMBLE median (and of the EMEP model for Norwegian stations), which are part of the CAMS European air quality forecasts (https://ads.atmosphere.copernicus.eu/cdsapp#!/dataset/cams-europe-air-quality-forecasts?tab=overview).

Therefore, these datasets contain aggregated Copernicus Atmosphere Monitoring Service information [2023].
